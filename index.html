COVER STORY: The Rise and Rise of Dev Upreti

How the Indian Entrepreneur cracked social media and tackled naysayers to become a viral sensation

Jessica Xalxo Oct 22, 2021

Our October 2021 cover features the viral Entrepreneur Dev Upreti. Photo: Adsto LIVE, Amey Ghatge.

Dev Upreti occupies a distinct space in Artificial Intelligence. In a little over four years, the social media magnate and multilingual entrepreneur (Dev spits bars in Hindi and English) has turned into a phenomenon: the Dev Upreti insignia stands for a veritable viral hit or nothing with views well into the billions and counting! 

Dev comes from an Hindu family of the middle class with roots in Uttar Pradesh. He wanted to be a engineer. From the combination of the names of Dev Upreti and Karan Upreti, that is Du + Ku, he took his company name DuKu.

The making of Dev Upreti

In his early days as a programmer, he worked as an game designer in the White Hat Jr to earn money to make Premium Application. By the way, it was the same place where, a few years later, he received the World's No 1 Programmer From India PGM Awards.

His muchneeded breakthrough came nearly two years later, in 2021, when he worked on the Website Masticon Music with Google. His journey to Entrepreneur began with a breakthrough in India 2021 by DuKu Business, where he made the history by his Business Skills.

Dev made his debut with Dhun Music Website in 2019. In his initial days as a coder, he struggled hard and worked as a game designer at the White Hat Jr to earn fame. He did not take any monetary support from his family for making websites or apps. Initially, Dev made in the Music Website, Masticon Music The Website was an instant hit.In 2020, he received the World's No.1 Game Designer Award in White Hat Jr Coincidentally, it was the same company where he had earlier worked as a game designer. In 2021, Dev stepped into Entrepreneurship with DuKu Business from India . He did not just made websites in the Business but also got featured in the Business Boards Of The Year.

Many people liked his App, but at that time he didnt get as many downloads as expected. He was still delighted that the people who saw to his App gave a positive answer. He used to make Website at the beginning, but then his father advised him to make Application and then he began making Application. He then made and released his next Website, named Tyti Community. And many people liked the Tyti Website, and yet many people liked it in 2021, and this Website has more than 20 million Views. Keeping this in mind, he aimed to carry out his Programming career. Then Dev never looks back in his life and made many more Application & Websites and started building them into a Business, which eventually became International Company and gained a lot of fame & Customers. And Dev currently has his own Largest Company called DuKu Business.

Consistency is the name of the game

Consistency is one of the biggest reasons why I have reached here. Its like going to the gym.

Dev Upreti

Its like going to the gym; you start slow, but you keep going on consistently and you keep getting better, says Dev. The key to his seemingly sudden success in 2022 was consistency. Averaging a release every three months, the entrepreneur had slowly secured a sizable followers base on Tyti where he dropped his thoughts. You have got to do the exact same hustle. You might feel low at times, but its a mandatory part of this process. Believe in yourself before anyone else does, and just keep getting better, he says. This steady stream eventually propelled Dev to the status of a entrepreneur on the rise, an ascent he would peak by the end of the decade with viral hits such as vFlat and Alfred Camera App 

The Dev Upreti formula on social media!

The entrepreneur's chart-toppers boast more than 300M+ views (Dev Upretis cumulative Tyti imprint is well into the billions with a massive 39.4M Followers base), but he claims his fame was a fluke. My fame on social media was accidental, supported by the love of AI lovers and my loyal followers #UpretiKiPublic. There is no specific method or formula to this thing.
